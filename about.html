EXPERTS IN LUXURY EVENT MANAGEMENT

A world-renowned boutique wedding and event management company that has become synonymous with destination weddings and private events, Yes, I Do Wedding Agency celebrates life, love and luxury. Understanding and appreciating the importance of life’s precious moments, Managing Director Cindy Moriarty has successfully sculpted her passion for weddings and events into a true art form.

With deep roots in the world of weddings and events, Cindy and her team has curated some of Ireland’s most coveted luxury events, both nationally and internationally. Celebrating over 20 years in the events industry, Cindy has worked with a cohort of some of the most accomplished conceptual artists in the business, forging solid relationships within the industry and assembling an enviable book of contacts to call upon.

INTERNATIONAL EVENT PLANNERS

AND SO THE ADVENTURE BEGINS...

Based in Ireland, Yes, I Do Wedding Agency was established to elevate event experiences for clients from all around the world. Designing and producing weddings and events for global clientele, Cindy’s unwavering promise to her clients knows no bounds. Believing there is a solution to every situation, her charismatic charm and discreet diplomacy ensures everything is possible and nothing is off limits.

Recognised as an inspiring and ambitious business woman, Cindy’s accolades and achievements are a true testament to the business she has created. Renowned amongst her peers as a formidable industry authority, Cindy’s expertise and extensive events experience has catapulted her brand to the forefront of the luxury events arena. Possessing the core business acumen skills required to build a world-class brand, Cindy regularly shares her knowledge and insights as guest speaker at international events. Her ability to carefully articulate her clients stories and deliver majestic masterpieces truly are a sight to behold.

While confidentiality, privacy, discretion and exclusivity are the primary elements of the brand’s success, Cindy and her team are very comfortable working within a multitude of different dynamics. Mastering the art of managing multiple time zones, looking after clients from Australia, China, New York and Europe, Yes, I Do Wedding Agency is discreetly spearheading the way as a global event specialist company.

“We love helping our clients to celebrate the extraordinary! They choose us to create a cohesive aesthetic and experience that is reflective of their personalities. We can do this by working on a select number of events each year to ensure we deliver the very best to our clients. We have been blessed with the honour and privilege of working in some of Ireland’s most spectacular venues and our diverse portfolio is a testament to the unique and bespoke service we offer our clients.”

- Cindy Moriarty, Managing Director of Yes, I Do Wedding Agency

Our wedding and event planning process

See your vision - be part of the process

It starts with a story: one that inspires us to plan, design, create, and produce every step of the way. We adapt our processes because we understand that there is no one size that fits all. We connect, we listen and we understand the importance of building an event from the ground up.

Each event and experience requires its own unique approach. Everyone thinks differently, sees differently and perceives differently. At the core of every event design is a thoughtful and integrated approach to our client’s style and personality. How do you want to feel? What do you want to see? Our design decks will transform your vision into reality as you see your event come to life.

Our detailed timelines act as transparent stepping stones as we co-ordinate and communicate with you every step of the way. Our conceptual artists’ design capabilities go beyond belief to create bespoke experiences that are true and accurate reflections of your dream design. A carefully curated design should represent your personal perfection by encompassing elements of the venue, destination and personalities. Every detail of our events are creatively composed to feel timeless and ethereal.

We understand that it is all about you. Let us do the work, all you have to do is sit back and enjoy the party!
