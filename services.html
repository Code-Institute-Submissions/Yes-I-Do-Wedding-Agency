“THE BEST THING TO HOLD ONTO IN LIFE IS EACH OTHER.”

From the moment you choose Yes I Do Wedding Agency, you can be assured that all aspects of your wedding are being looked after by an experienced wedding professional. We take care of all the details, locate all the special services that suit perfectly to our couple’s vision of their big day, and work with you to design an event that is unique, sophisticated and highly personal!

Basic Planner Package

This service suits couples who have already started planning their wedding but require assistance to pull it all together. We help complete the planning process, source missing suppliers, develop a wedding day schedule and execute the details on the day.

Inclusions:

  • Unlimited phone and email communication one month from your event
  • Up to 3 planning sessions including one ceremony and venue walkthrough
  • Access to our preferred vendors lists for any booking requirements that are still outstanding
  • Vendor confirmation one month before your wedding
  • Creation of a wedding day timeline and schedule of events
  • Creation of an event production schedule for vendors
  • Coordination of your wedding rehearsal the evening before your wedding
  • Set up and set down of any décor, prop and feature installations that have been provided*
  • Onsite coordination and management of all events and wedding suppliers
  • Outstanding vendor payments
Complate Planner Package

The Complete Planner Package is ideally suited to busy couples who just want someone to take care of everything. We work with you to shape a budget, choose a collaborative team of carefully selected suppliers, style your wedding and manage the day onsite

Inclusions:

  • Unlimited Hours consultation
  • Assistance sourcing and securing wedding venue
  • Assistance with legal paperwork
  • Budget management with our bespoke budget management spreadsheet
  • Administration Management
  • Assistance with all vendor shortlisting, selection, negotiation and contracts
  • Act as point of contact for all vendors before, on and up to 7 days after the wedding day
  • Create a vendor payment schedule
  • Schedule vendor appointments
  • Attend vendor appointments on your behalf if necessary
  • Create Master To-Do List
  • Manage Tasks
  • Creation of a wedding day timeline and schedule of events
  • Creation of an event production schedule for vendors
  • Coordination of your wedding rehearsal the evening before your wedding
  • Oversee production on wedding day
  • Onsite coordination and management of all events and wedding suppliers
  • Outstanding vendor payments
Wedding Styling & Design

You have organized everything by yourself, but want to have the peace of mind that all the details on your big day will come together as you envisioned. After a consultation with the couple about one month prior to the wedding, we assemble a detailed timeline for the wedding day including the responsibilities of all persons involved to ensure that everything runs smooth. Your Wedding Planner will be onsite and supervise the event from set up to the first dance, so you’ll be able to truly enjoy every moment of the day!

Inclusions:

  • Unlimited phone and email communication one month from your event
  • Up to 3 planning sessions including one ceremony and venue walkthrough
  • Access to our preferred vendors lists for any booking requirements that are still outstanding
  • Vendor confirmation one month before your wedding
  • Creation of a wedding day timeline and schedule of events
